Josicon Engineering

Josicon Engineering Plc Company is a sole proprietor established in 2009 E.C. , licensed as B.C. contractors, registered for VAT, and has domestic investment license. It is engaged in construction of civil projects, gaining a good reputation due to its efficiency and innovation working methodologies.

Vision of the company

To become one of the most competitive company in the country through innovation, improvement, perfection integrity, and advanced performance and to be the preeminent provider of superior construction service to our customer.

Mission of the company

To provide, for our customers the highest level of quality construction service at fair and market competitive prices both technical and management aspect.
To maintain the highest levels of professionalism, integrity, honesty and fairness in our relationships with our client, subcontractors.

Construction

We are a team of experienced professionals dedicated to providing high-quality construction services to our clients. Our services include new construction, renovations, and remodeling projects for both residential and commercial properties. We pride ourselves on our attention to detail and commitment to customer satisfaction.
